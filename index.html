The Problem

When you walk into an art museum, do you feel out-of-place? Intimidated? Like that space isn’t meant for you? You’re not alone. Everyone has thoughts and feelings about art, but often people feel that their art opinions are inferior and not worth sharing. That’s where Amarteurs comes in.

  • Learn more

Our Solution

With the Amarteur Hour mobile web app, you can anonymously share your emotional reactions to museum art pieces and see how many people felt the same way. You can also leave comments and see comments from people who shared the same emotional response.

The web app can also point you to multiple large interactive displays scattered around the museum.

Here, you can view how many people reacted to any piece of art in different ways.

Your art opinions matter and are shared by others, and Amarteurs helps you connect with the huge community of art amateurs.
