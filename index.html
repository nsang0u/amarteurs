Art art art...

When you walk into an art museum, do you feel out-of-place? Intimidated? Like that space isn’t meant for you? You’re not alone. Everyone has thoughts and feelings about art, but often people feel that their art opinions are inferior and not worth sharing. That’s where

Amarteurs

comes in.

  • Learn more

Our Solution

With the Amarteurs web app, you can anonymously share your emotional reactions to museum art pieces and see how many people felt the same way.

(PICTURE OF “HERES ALL THE PEOPLE WHO SELECTED HAPPY” SCREEN)

You can also leave comments and see comments from people who shared the same emotional response.

(PICTURE OF “HERE ARE COMMENTS FROM USERS WITH THE SAME RESPONSE” PAGE).

The web app can also point you to multiple large interactive displays scattered around the museum.

Here, you can view how many people reacted to any piece of art in different ways. (PICTURE OF CROWD OF SILHOUETTES WITH REACTION CHOICES SCREEN) You can also see and respond to any comments from all users.

(PICTURE OF BOARD COMMENTS SCREEN).

Your art opinions matter and are shared by others, and Amarteurs helps you connect with the huge community of art amateurs.
